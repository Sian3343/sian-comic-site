關於我｜About Me

This is the inner world I recorded through daily life.
I’m Sian, a creator diagnosed with Asperger’s Syndrome as an adult.
I’m used to expressing myself with drawings or words instead of speech, using comics to portray the bitter sweetness of life.
If you’ve ever felt out of place in society, I hope my stories bring you some resonance and companionship.
