關於我｜About Me

這是我從生活點滴記下的內心世界。
我是 Sian，是成人確診 Asperger's 的創作者。
我習慣以畫筆或文字，代替說話，用漫畫記錄生活中的酸甜苦辣。

These comics reflect the world I see from my inner lens.
I’m Sian, an adult diagnosed with Asperger's Syndrome.
I use drawing and writing to speak in place of words, capturing the ups and downs of everyday life.
