我是 Sian，有著 Asperger 特質，喜歡用漫畫記錄生活裡的細膩感受與心情片段。
我喜歡把日子轉化為線條，用文字留下腳步——慢慢走，也慢慢畫。
如果想更認識我，可以點進「關於我」 👩🏻

I’m Sian. I’m on the autism spectrum (Asperger’s) and I express my everyday thoughts and feelings through comics.
I love turning life into lines and words—walking slowly, drawing slowly, feeling deeply.
If you'd like to know more, head over to “About Me” 👩🏻
