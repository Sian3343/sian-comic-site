關於我｜About Me

你好，我係 Sian，一位熱愛創作嘅漫畫人。

這是我從生活點滴記下的內心世界。
我是成人確診 Asperger's 的創作者。
我習慣以畫筆或文字代替說話，用漫畫記錄生活中的酸甜苦辣。

──

Hello, I’m Sian — a storyteller and comic creator from the inside out.

These comics reflect the world I see through my inner lens.
Diagnosed with Asperger’s as an adult, I speak not only with words, but with drawings and comics.
Every stroke and story is a piece of my lived experience.
