關於我｜About Me

嗨，我是 Sian。
是用畫筆記錄生活的漫畫創作者，也是一位成人確診 Asperger's。
對我而言，世界有些複雜，人與人之間的距離，總讓我難以捉摸。
說話不是我的強項，但透過繪畫與書寫，我能更真實地表達內心的聲音。

我畫的漫畫，不只是日常的紀錄，也是自我療癒的過程。
畫筆是我與世界對話的方式，它幫助我理清思緒，轉化情緒，也讓我在孤單中找到一些理解。

這些故事都源自真實生活，沒有誇張與修飾，只是靜靜地訴說那些不被看見的感受。
如果你也曾在某些時刻覺得不被理解，或者感到格格不入，
希望這裡的每一幅圖，都能給你一點共鳴與陪伴。

Hi, I’m Sian.
I’m a comic artist who expresses life through drawing, and an adult with Asperger’s Syndrome.
The world often feels complex to me, and human interactions can be hard to navigate.
Speaking isn’t my strength—but through art and writing, I can share my thoughts more truthfully.

My comics are more than daily snapshots; they are part of my journey to heal and understand myself.
Drawing is how I connect with the world—it helps me process emotions and find clarity within the chaos.

Each story is based on real experiences.
There’s no filter, no exaggeration—just quiet expressions of feelings that are often overlooked.
If you’ve ever felt misunderstood or out of place,
I hope my drawings offer you a sense of resonance and gentle companionship.
